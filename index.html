BubbleTrouble
How To Play
×
  1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move your character
  2. Press space to shoot your weapon at the bubbles!
  3. Destroy all the bubbles before the timer runs out!
  4. Bubbles split when hit, and will continue splitting until they are too small
  5. Avoid the bubbles, you will lose a life when you collide with a bubble
